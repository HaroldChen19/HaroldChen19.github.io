Biography

I'm a 1st-year PhD student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Ying-Cong Chen and Prof. Qifeng Chen. I obtained my B.E. degree at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU) with Outstanding Graduate Award in 2025, where I worked closely with Prof. Dian Shao. I'm also fortunate to work closely with Prof. Harry Yang and Prof. Ser-Nam Lim.

I am always open to all forms of research collaboration. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in working with me! My research interests include Video Generation & Understanding.
